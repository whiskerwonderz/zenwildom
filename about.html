About

The story behind the lens

Photography, for me, is an act of stillness and presence. The same intention we bring to the mat, to meditation, and to our inward journey. That deep attention to the present moment is what I carry behind the camera.

My path as a teacher began in Costa Rica in 2020. Since then, I have guided yoga across continents—Panama, Ecuador, Portugal, Hong Kong and Thailand. I have also trained to guide breathwork, gong baths and the Navakaraṇa methodology, deepening my holistic practice. These practices shape how I see, listen, capture moments and curate stories.

Somewhere along the journey, photography became my practice.

I work with yoga teachers and studios, eco-lodges and retreat hosts, holistic wellness and earth-inspired brands. My approach is mindful and intuitive, I focus on documenting what is already there—beautifully unique movements to moments.

When I am not behind the camera, you will find me in my own practice and explorations—wild mountains, quiet islands, sunkissed rice paddies—wherever stillness and zenness meets the wild and untamed. Always drawn to the roads less travelled.

If this resonates, I would love to connect and document your work.

With light and love,

Crystal
