About

The story behind the lens

Your portrait here

Photography, for me, is about presence. The same quality we cultivate on the mat—that deep attention to the present moment—is what I bring behind the camera.

I work with yoga teachers, retreat leaders, and wellness brands who want imagery that feels real. No forced poses or artificial setups. Just honest, beautiful documentation of the transformative work you do.

When I'm not shooting, you'll find me on my own mat, usually somewhere with a view of rice paddies.
